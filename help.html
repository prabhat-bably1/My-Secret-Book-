Help Center

How do I save text in my profile?

Profile page par jaakar left side me text likho, phir “Save Secret” button dabao. Saved text neeche Word tab me dikh jayega.

How do I save images?

Profile page me image choose karo aur “Save Image” button dabao. Saved image Images tab me dikh jayegi.

What does Public Wall do?

Public Wall par users pyar, shayari, jokes ya sweet messages likh sakte hain. Guest posts temporary rehte hain.

What is Notice Board?

Notice Board me public Word aur Image posts ek board style layout me dikhte hain.

Why are some words auto changed?

Abuse filter harmful, sexual ya offensive words ko automatically soft loving words me replace kar deta hai.

Need Support?

Agar aapko help chahiye to apna message bhejiye. Request browser me save ho jayegi.
